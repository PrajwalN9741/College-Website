Our Courses

Courses We Offer

We offer Intermediate (PUC) and Degree programs including BCA, B.Com, BBA, and B.Sc, designed to provide quality education and practical skills for a successful career.

Intermediate (PUC)

Streams: Science (PCMB/PCMCs), Commerce (EBACs), Arts.
A two-year pre-university course that builds a strong foundation for higher education.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications
Focuses on programming, software development, web design, and database management to prepare students for the IT industry.

B.Com

Bachelor of Commerce
Covers accounting, finance, taxation, and business law, ideal for aspiring accountants and financial analysts.

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration
Develops leadership, management, and entrepreneurial skills for business and corporate roles.

B.Sc

Bachelor of Science
Offers specializations in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Computer Science for research and technical careers.

BA

Bachelor of Arts
History, Economics, Political Science. Fosters critical thinking and understanding of society and humanities.
