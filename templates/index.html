Courses We Offer

We offer Intermediate (PUC) and Degree programs including BCA, B.Com, BBA, and B.Sc, designed to provide quality education and practical skills for a successful career.

Intermediate

The National College offers Intermediate (PUC) courses in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. Our programs are designed to build a strong academic foundation with expert guidance, modern facilities, and a focus on both board exam success and career readiness.

Degree

The National College offers a variety of undergraduate degree programs including BCA, B.Com, BBA, and B.Sc. Our courses focus on academic excellence, skill development, and career-oriented training to prepare students for the future.

Our College Campus

The National College campus offers a vibrant and student-friendly environment with spacious classrooms, modern labs, a well-stocked library, and excellent sports and recreational facilities. It’s a perfect blend of academics and campus life that supports overall student growth.
